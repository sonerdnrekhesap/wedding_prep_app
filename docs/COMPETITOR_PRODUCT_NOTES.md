# Competitor Product Notes

Date: 22 July 2026

## Signals Used

- The Knot emphasizes personalized checklist, vendors, guest organization, RSVP, registry, budget and a shared planning account.
- WeddingWire emphasizes checklist, vendor manager, wedding website, budget, seating chart, guest list and RSVP.
- Zola emphasizes guest list, RSVP, contact collection, budget tracker, seating chart, export and registry.

## Product Direction For Hazirlik Takibi

Hazirlik Takibi should not copy the US vendor/registry-heavy model directly. The stronger local wedge is:

- Offline-first ceyiz, dugun, bohca and balayi checklist.
- Budget and missing item clarity.
- Guest list export and family sharing.
- Calm weekly plan instead of a crowded dashboard.
- Premium value only after the user sees free utility.

## Premium Positioning

Free value stays useful:

- Checklist
- Budget basics
- Guest list
- CSV share
- Weekly plan preview

Premium offer should focus on:

- Ad-free planning
- PDF/Excel exports
- Deeper budget and missing-item analysis
- Unlimited photo/receipt archive
- Premium summary cards
- Partner sync foundation

## Splash / First Impression

High-retention planning apps avoid a blank technical startup. The implemented direction:

- Native branded background.
- Centered app mark.
- Flutter warm-start animation with brand promise.
- Short, calm motion rather than a loud marketing splash.
